Marta Soare

Currently at Université Grenoble Alpes and LIG, as visiting researcher (délégation CNRS) in the SLIDE team.

Since 2017, I have been an Associate Professor (Maître de Conférences) at Université d'Orléans and LIFO. I obtained my PhD from Université Lille 1, as member of the SequeL team at Inria Lille (2012-2015). I was a postdoctoral researcher at Aalto University, in the Probabilistic Machine Learning group (2015-2017).

Research interests: secure machine learning, multi-armed bandit problems, active and interactive learning, machine learning for precision medicine
